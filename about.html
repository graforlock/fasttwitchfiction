Necrofiction

In the time it takes
to flinch

Fast Twitch Fiction is a horror flash-fiction magazine for the short, wet, and unspeakable.

We publish necrofiction.

What is necrofiction, you might ask? It's horror, it's body horror, it's cosmic grotesque, whatever divine or diseased stuff that floats your boat. And every piece is flash: it must be read in a single twitch. Like the contraction of a fast muscle before your brain has caught up.

The stories are published in the magazine, which is published quarterly. There are awards for the top three pieces.

The hope and the promise of the magazine is that it expands into galaxies, black branes and into the very consciousness of God. Oh, how he'll scorn us.
